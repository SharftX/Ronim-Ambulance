Saving Lives Since 2006 – Your Trusted Lifeline Across Sri Lanka

At Ronim Ambulance Services, we are greater than simply an ambulance service; we're a crew of devoted specialists committed to saving lives and ensuring the well-being of every affected person we serve. Why Choose Us?

When every second counts, our ambulance service is a trusted choice for fast and reliable emergency medical care. With over 19 years of experience, Ronim Ambulance provides life-saving medical transport across Sri Lanka with speed, precision, and compassion, while also supporting patient recovery through our professional home nursing service when continued care is needed.

  • 19 Years of Excellence: Our ambulance service brings more than 18 years of proven expertise in emergency medical transportation, earning trust through professionalism, reliability, and high-quality patient care during transport and continued recovery through our home nursing service.
  • 24/7 Emergency Availability: Emergencies never wait. Our ambulance service operates 24/7, ensuring immediate response at any hour with well-equipped vehicles and trained medical teams, while supporting patients with home nursing service when follow-up care is required.
  • Islandwide Ambulance Coverage: We provide islandwide ambulance service across Sri Lanka, reaching busy cities and remote areas, and assisting patients who may later need professional home nursing service for safe recovery at home.
  • Professional and Compassionate Medical Team: Our experienced paramedics, medical staff, and drivers are dedicated to patient safety, comfort, and dignity throughout every journey, with the same standard of care extended through our trusted home nursing service.
  • Complete Care Beyond Transport: In addition to emergency and non-emergency ambulance service, we offer a reliable home nursing service to support patient recovery, post-hospital care, and ongoing medical assistance in a comfortable home environment.

Our goal is to deliver quality of care in a courteous, respectful, and compassionate manner. We hope you will allow us to care for you and strive to be the first and best choice for your family healthcare.
